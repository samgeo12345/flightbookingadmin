Help Section

Welcome to the help section. Here you'll find answers to common questions about using our airline booking system. Whether you need guidance on booking flights or managing passenger details, we're here to help you navigate our platform smoothly.

Adding a Passenger

To add a new passenger to the system, navigate to the form titled "Add Passenger". Fill in the required fields including Name, Email, Country, State, Phone Number, Gender, and Address. Once all fields are filled, click the "Add Passenger" button to save the passenger details. The passenger will be added to the list and can be viewed in the passengers section.

Searching for a Passenger

To search for a specific passenger, use the search bar provided on the customer page. Enter any relevant information such as the passenger's name, email, country. Click the search button, and if a matching passenger is found, their details will be displayed in the same page and refresh the page to make normal.

Updating Passenger Details

If you need to update the details of an existing passenger, ask to him/her id of the passenger.Click the "Update Passenger" button to save the updated information. The passenger's details will be updated in the system and reflected in the passengers list.

Deleting a Passenger

To delete a passenger, search the passenger in the passengers list. Each passenger entry will have a "Delete" button next to it. Click this button to remove the passenger from the system. Please note that this action cannot be undone, so ensure that you really want to delete the passenger before proceeding.

Adding Flight Details

To add flight details of the specific passenger, click the Flight Details" in the home section. Enter the flight information, including passenger id,flight number, brand, model, seat number, origin, destination, arrival time, and departure time, and click the button to save the flight details.

See Flight Details

To see the flight details of a passenger, click the "Flight" button next to the passenger's entry in the passengers list. This will take you to a page where you can view flight details specific to that passenger.
